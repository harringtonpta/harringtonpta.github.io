Message from PTA Co-Presidents

Welcome, Hawk families, to this unique and challenging 2020-2021 school year.

The Harrington PTA exists for the benefit of our children. Our teams of parent volunteers work closely with our Harrington principal, staff, and teachers to ensure the welfare of our children at school and throughout our community. As a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, we rely upon our membership and school-wide fundraising opportunities to deliver new learning experiences for our students, boost social-emotional wellness connections, and support our teams of educators so they can focus on the work of teaching our children.

Family Connections: Understand what’s happening in our school by staying connected via the PTA Membership Toolkit emails, this website, Facebook, and Twitter. Room Parents and our many parent volunteers who run our programs and events will continue serving our students by connecting families throughout this 2020-2021 school year.

Great Programs: The PTA delivers programs that enhance our children’s learning opportunities, and we’ll continue to do so this year. We work with curriculum directors to bring programs like Arts Created Together, Science Enrichment, Big Backyard, Math Mornings, Map Night, and so many others to the entire school throughout the year.

Our Harrington PTA strives to speak as one voice for all our Harrington Hawks. Please do contact us at HarringtonConnects@gmail.com with any questions you may have.

Thank you for your support!

Melissa Lee and Keri Caron
PTA Co-Presidents
HarringtonConnects@gmail.com
