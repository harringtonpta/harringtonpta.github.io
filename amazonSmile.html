Start at Smile.Amazon.com to support Harrington PTA

When you go to smile.amazon.com for your shopping, Amazon uses money saved on advertising to help fund donations to charities. To earn money for Harrington, remember to "start with a smile." It's more than a slogan. We mean it literally. Put the word smile in front of amazon.com to support Harrington School PTA.

Worried you'll forget to type smile? Bookmark smile.amazon.com to create a shortcut to quickly return to AmazonSmile from your desktop or mobile browser.

Haven’t set up your Amazon Smile yet?

  1. Go to smile.amazon.com and sign in
  2. Type “Harrington School” in the "Pick your own charitable organization” box
  3. Select Harrington School PTA, Lexington MA on the next page
  4. Bookmark smile.amazon.com and start shopping
