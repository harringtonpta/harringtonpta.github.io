RECYCLE Your Household Textiles!

Baystate Textile Program

Drop off old textiles, stuffed animals, old rugs, linens, clothes, shoes and more… to any of the two boxes in the Harrington parking lot & Harrington will receive funds based on how much we donate. It’s simple, provides funds to our PTA, and helps reuse items to keep them out of the trash.

This program’s focus is to spread awareness and educate the community on the problem of textile waste. To learn more visit Baystate Textiles Website.

COMPOSTING for Hawks

Looking for a way to recycle more of your waste AND help out your child’s school. Mention “Harrington PTA” in the referral section when you sign up for curbside pick-up from Black Earth Compost – and the Harrington PTA will receive a $10 donation! Learn more about why you’d want to do this here.
