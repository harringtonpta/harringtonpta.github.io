Committee Chairperson Description
A.C.T.
(Arts Created Together)		 Kristen Reed
Stacey Smiar		 ACT Chairs collaborate with Principal and staff to find and schedule arts enrichment program for each grade's curriculum. There are 2 school wide presentations as well as 2 additional presentations per grade. These programs are funded 100% by the PTA
Big Backyard Tarla NeffK
Jana Hersch		 This is a town-wide parent run science enrichment program. The program is developed to enrich each grade's science curriculum. 3 Parent volunteers from each classroom participate in 1 hour training per season. Will then lead students on seasonal explorations behind the school during class time several times throughout the school year.
Science Enrichment Kristen Reed
Stacey Smiar		 This PTA funded program brings grade-level, curriculum based science programming to all Harrington students.

Fund Raising

Committee Chairperson Description
Stop & Shop OPEN Register your Stop & Shop card and earn cash for Harrington every time you shop!
Harrington Hurrah Kristin Bjork It's a PARTY for the Harrington Community! Parents and children come to school on a Friday night for family evening of food, games and silent auction.
Harrington Spring Fair Judith Sanders??
OPEN		 The Spring Fair is a beloved tradition at Harrington, as well as the PTA's biggest fund-raiser. The Fair is an exciting event for the entire family
School/Family Picture Days Seiza Picture days take place in the Fall and include student and class photos as well as a fundraising family portrait event
Fall Fundraiser Julie Fuhrer Many different products for sale beyond gift wrap including gifts and gourmet food. 50% of total sales fund Harrington PTA initiatives.
Box Tops Kris Palomba This PTA Fundraiser encourages students collect General Mills Box Tops and bring them into the lobby every Tuesday morning to help supplement the PTA General Fund. Small prizes are awarded to all students.
Barnes and Noble Christine?? This PTA Fundraiser is sponsered by Barnes and Noble Bookstore. Students are encouraged to read and buy books at Barnes and Noble in a specied time so that a portion of the proceeds are donated to Harrington School. Harrington teachers volunteer their time to read at the store.
Spirit Wear Emy Goldsmith This PTA Fundraiser encourages students to show school spirit by purchasing T-shirts, sweatshirts, sweatpants and/or hats with the Harrington School name and mascot.
Wilson Farm Day Karin Arnold This PTA Fundraiser is sponsered by Wilson Farm in Lexington. Harrington families are encouraged to shop at Wilson farm on a specified day and the farm donates part of the proceedes from sales to the Harrington PTA.

School Support

Committee Chairperson Description
Library Volunteers Newcomers and experienced volunteers are welcome - and needed! Training is available. It's a fun and relaxing way to get to know the students and staff, while providing a valuable service to your child's class. You can help once a week or once a month, your choice!
Green Team Tarla Neff
Juliana Lyman		 The Green Team is a group of parent volunteers interested and passionate about preserving our environment, increasing education and awareness at school and home on environmental issues and making your child's home and school Greener places to live. This team is working with Harrington staff and administration to assess all current green efforts at Harrington, they work with other Lexington schools (particularly elementary schools), the DPW and the town to increase awareness and decrease the school's carbon footprint through various programming and interventions.
Room Parents Coordinator Karin Arnold Head Room Parents, with assistance from the Room Parents, work to facilitate communication between teachers and parents. They coordinate volunteers for the Big Backyard, organize class baskets, plan class parties, welcome new families; and assist teachers with other class events/activities.
Community Service Coordinator Not this year Work with Principal & teachers to plan at least one community service project for students to participate in during the school year
Map Night Maria/Tarl
Lost and Found Elaine Ashtona The Lost & Found is located next to the Gym near the Kindergarten wing. Labeled items are returned to students; unclaimed items are returned at the end of the year.
5th Grade Graduation OPEN 5th grade parents collaborate with 5th grade teachers to plan the camping trip, graduation ceremony, DVD, yearbook, pool party & class gift. They also work with students to fundraise for end of year events.
Math Mornings/Night Jennifer Roney
Vlad Bulovic		 Math Nights are parent-child fun filled evening of math games held once a year for each grade. Math mornigns provide math enrichment program run by parents to build problem -solving skills. For 3rd, 4th, and 5th graders, held once a week before school.
Harrington Reads Book Club Claire Sheth This parent/child book club is open to all 4th and 5th graders. Meetings are facilitated by the committee chairs and are held twice a year for each grade.
Beautification Leslie Zales
Maria Vlachos
