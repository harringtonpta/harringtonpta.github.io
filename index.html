PTA Updates

Feb. 25th Kindness Rocks Event

Join us on Thursday, Feb. 25th for Harrington’s Kindness Rocks event! This will be a chance for students and families to have some fun together while creating great messages.
Date: Thursday, Feb. 25
Time: 5 pm-5:30 pm: Support a local restaurant, get some take-out, and join us to connect and “eat together” with other Harrington families. 5:30pm: The event officially begins … and we decorate our rocks together!
Cost: $5 per family. Each Take-Home kit will have 6 pre-painted rocks.
To Join: Register through the Store/Events in your Membership Toolkit Account by:
Wednesday, February 3rd. https://harringtonelem.membershiptoolkit.com/.

COVID-19 Testing in Lex

Good News! Students and staff have been taking the screening tests for COVID-19 and it's helping us keep a safer learning environment in LPS. See the results here.

Lexington Education Foundation (LEF) is teaming up with LPS to help raise funds for this effort. They say the more we can raise, the longer this programming can stay in place for the community. If you’d like to donate to the COVID-19 surveillance testing program or to find out more about it, click here.

Connecting with the PTA

If you’ve wondered what’s happening or would like to connect with the PTA, feel free to join our regular monthly PTA Board meeting on Thursday, February 4th at 7 pm. Any PTA member who wishes to attend can email HarringtonConnects@gmail.com for access prior to 5 pm on Feb. 4th.

Welcome to Our New Website!

We’re thrilled to share the new Harrington PTA website with you as a one-stop place to find information or connect! Thank you to our web team of seven talented high school students, who responded to a call for help on this project and spent their weekends learning state-of-the-art technology. Thank you to our very own VP of Communication, Dr. Wei Ding, who took the time and shared her expertise with these students! See Dr. Hackett’s remarks about this collaboration from her Dec. 8th report!
