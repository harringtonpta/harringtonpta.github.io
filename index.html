PTA Updates

Outdoor Spaces – Please Help Us Create Safe Learning & Play Spaces at School!

We’re currently raising funds to help Harrington increase our outside options during this pandemic. The PTA will purchase outdoor canopies, more than 100 lap desks, safe play options, and increase outdoor spaces for all of our students and teachers to enjoy. Please consider donating to this fund via Membership Toolkit. (Visit Store and click on Outdoor Donations.) Any amount will go directly to provide outdoor teaching options for our teachers, socially distanced games, and creating new play spaces. These purchases help keep our school community safe during the pandemic, but they will be useful for all future Harrington activities.

Share Our Moments: The Hawk Monthly

Please send in your photos of what school looks like for you and your family this year. Our Hawk Monthly editor Jane Reynolds will be collecting our community photos and other fun tidbits to share with our school. It’s one simple way for us all to stay connected in this virtual world.

Please send your photos to: janearey11@gmail.com and label your child’s name, grade, and teacher. We welcome all photos, and would especially love:
- Pictures from new community members. If you are new to Harrington this year, please consider this a chance to introduce yourselves! Include the name of your previous school or city if you like.
- Halloween pictures! For those who celebrate, let's see those amazing costumes! We plan to send out the first issue of the Monthly right after Halloween.

Math Mornings Are On!

Harrington Math Mornings for Grades 3, 4, and 5 focus on strengthening problem-solving skills and help our kids discover how powerful, diverse, and fun math can be. Groups meet once a week before school from Oct. thru May. All students are welcome!
Please register here to join!
3rd Grade: Fridays 8:00-8:30 am
4th Grade: Mondays 8:00-8:30 am
5th Grade: Wednesdays 8:00-8:30 am
Any questions or wish to help? Contact the PTA Math Morning Chair at Sona.Antonyan@russianschool.com.
