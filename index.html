Welcome to Harrington Elementary School!

The Harrington Parent Teacher Association is a vibrant organization of parent volunteers with a mission to promote the health and quality education of our children and foster collaboration between parents, the Harrington school staff and the community. As a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, we rely upon our membership and school-wide fundraising opportunities to sustain educational programs, help deliver new learning experiences to our students, boost social- emotional wellness with a focus on inclusion, and support all Harrington educators.

Your membership in the PTA will help fund great programs and events. We work with curriculum directors to bring programs like Arts Created Together, Science Enrichment, Big Backyard, Math Mornings, Map Night, and so many others to the entire school throughout the year. To learn more about our programs click (Take them to the Program Page)

Stay connected to the PTA and school activities via the PTA Membership Toolkit emails, this website, Facebook, and Twitter. Become a Room Parent and/or join our many parent volunteers who run our programs and events throughout the year. If you are interested in leadership opportunities or developing a new program for the school, we would love to hear from you!

We are all Harrington Hawks and we look forward to seeing you and supporting your children during this 2024/2025 academic year. Please don’t hesitate to contact us at president.harringtonelempta@gmail.com with any questions you may have.
