PTA Updates

Outdoor Spaces – Please Help Us Create Safe Learning & Play Spaces at School!

Increasing our time outdoors is one of our goals during this pandemic. The PTA is working closely with Principal Daley and LPS to find safe ways to allow our teachers to bring classes and play time outside as much as possible. Please consider donating to this fund via Membership Toolkit. (Visit Store and click on Outdoor Donations.) Any amount will go directly to provide outdoor teaching options for our teachers (canopy tents, desks, seating), socially distanced games (blacktop paints, etc.), and creating new play spaces (potential for new playground structures). The PTA will dedicate these funds to help keep our students and our school community safer during the pandemic while ensuring we have sustainability plans in place for future use.

Room Parents

Become a Room Parent for your child’s classroom! Room Parents help teachers to connect families in each classroom. And we thank all who have signed up to help this year. Our plan is to continue teacher appreciation throughout the year and share interesting ways to connect our kids across hybrids and RLA. If you think you can help, please sign up for your class here. We’re still looking for Room Parents for a couple of classes!

Share Our Moments: The Hawk Monthly

The first issue of The Hawk Monthly is here! Please send in your photos of what school looks like for you and your family this year. Our Hawk Monthly editor Jane Reynolds will be collecting our community photos and other fun tidbits to share with our school every month. It’s one simple way for us all to stay connected in this virtual world.

Send your photos to: janearey11@gmail.com and label your child’s name, grade, and teacher.

Math Mornings Are On!

Harrington Math Mornings for Grades 3, 4, and 5 focus on strengthening problem-solving skills and help our kids discover how powerful, diverse, and fun math can be. Groups meet once a week before school from Oct. thru May. All students are welcome!
Please register here to join!

4th Grade: Mondays 8:00-8:30 am
5th Grade: Wednesdays 8:00-8:30 am
