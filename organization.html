Curriculum Support

Chairperson
Description
A.C.T (Arts Created Together)/Science
Stacey Smiar
Jennifer Zacharis
The PTA brings curriculum enrichment programs to our students throughout the year. This team works with our principal, teachers, and staff to provide art and science programming that directly supports each grade level. To see just a few of the programs the PTA delivered, click here.
Math Mornings & Math Nights
Sona Antonyan
This math enrichment program is run by parent volunteers to help 3rd, 4th, and 5th Graders to build problem-solving skills. Math mornings are typically held once a week. In 2020, all math sessions will be held virtually. Math nights occur for each grade once a year and are a fun-filled night for the entire family.
Big Backyard (BBY)
Gretchen Rabinkin
Julia Thompson
BBY is the District-wide, parent-run science enrichment outdoor program. It teaches the importance of exploring the outdoors while encouraging young learners to apply scientific questions and observations appropriate for each grade level. Volunteers for each classroom lead students on seasonal walks for science exploration.
Library Support
Bridget Koha
Library volunteers for each class typically support the school librarian during sessions. This year, our support volunteers will work to highlight great reading opportunities and help our teachers to keep reading exciting for our students.
Map Night
Jill Weng
The National Geographic Society Giant Map visits Harrington once a year. LPS and the PTA work together to surround this exciting opportunity with fun learning, crafts, and games.

Room Parents

Room Parents play a critical role in communications between teachers and families. They coordinate BBY, class celebrations, Spring Fair baskets, and welcome new families. Room parents can work together to help build classroom connections.
5th Grade Graduation
TBD
5th Grade Parents team up with 5th Grade teachers and a 5th Grade student-run committee to plan and fundraise for the graduation ceremony, camping trip, yearbook, pool party, class gift, and other social-building events for the 5th Grade class.
Teacher Appreciation
TBD
This PTA team helps to lead volunteers from every class and grade in celebrating our teachers all year long and specifically during Teacher Appreciation Week in May.
Safe Routes to School
Emily Sayre
This team works with the Town, LPS, and Harrington to ensure our students and families have safe access to walk and bike to school every day. Their efforts are helping us to get safer sidewalks and signage along Maple Street!
Green Team
Jill Ward
Our Green Team leads our volunteers in supporting school and Town-wide efforts for composting and recycling. The team works directly with our school, LPS, and DPW to reduce our school’s carbon footprint and enhance environmental education. Learn more here!
Outdoor Design (Beautification)
Kate Davis
This program assists our school in creating and maintaining spaces and opportunities to brighten our school environment or allow effective use of indoor and outdoor spaces. Bricks for sale, the meditation garden, and school playground projects fall under this PTA-run program.

METCO Family Friends
Sheila Lawrence
METCO family friends connects our METCO students with fellow students once a month for after-school meet ups. Lexington-resident families are matched with a Boston family to ensure we build and maintain strong relationships for all of our Harrington Hawks. The 2020-2021 school year is being planned with COVID safety in mind.

Spring Fair
TBD
Harrington’s Spring Fair is a beloved school tradition. Each spring, we come together to enjoy games, food, dunk tanks, magic shows and more as we wrap up the end of the school year. This is our school’s largest fundraiser to support our Arts Created Together & Science programs, our events, and the many school community efforts the PTA manages.
Fun Run
Lynne Sheeley
Jeannine Hunt Dickholtz
The Harrington Fun Run is a great school community event that helps us kick off the school year with exercise, fun, and lots of school spirit. Join friends, fellow Hawks, and our teachers as you race around a course set up around the school.
Fall Fundraiser
Yongting Wang
Every fall, the PTA raises funds through the sale of gifts, gourmet foods, wrapping papers, and other products that can make holiday gift giving much easier. This year the fundraiser offers direct delivery to your home for all orders. Up to 40% of total sales fund our Harrington PTA initiatives.
