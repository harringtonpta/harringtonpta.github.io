A.C.T. (Arts Created Together)

ACT Chairs collaborate with Principal and staff to find and schedule arts enrichment program for each grade's curriculum. There are 2 school wide presentations as well as 2 additional presentations per grade. These programs are funded 100% by the PTA.

Big Backyard

This is a town-wide parent run science enrichment program. The program is developed to enrich each grade's science curriculum. 3 Parent volunteers from each classroom participate in 1 hour training per season. Will then lead students on seasonal explorations behind the school during class time several times throughout the school year

Box Tops

This PTA Fundraiser encourages students to collect General Mills Box Tops and bring them into school to supplement the PTA General Fund. Small prizes are awarded to all students. Student can attach their box tops to this form and bring them into school. When using a plastic bag only please include student's name, teacher and total count.

Community Read Aloud

Lexington community leaders (such as police and fire personnel, business owners, elected officials) come to Harrington and read to the students one day in winter.

Family Math Nights/Mornings

Math Nights are parent-child fun filled evening of math games held once a year for each grade. Math mornigns provide math enrichment program run by parents to build problem -solving skills. For 3rd, 4th, and 5th graders,held once a week before school.

Family Sports Outings

The family sports outings are offered several times a year and include trips to see the Lowell Spinners and the Devils hockey team

Green Team

The Green Team is a group of parent volunteers that oversees the composting and recycling of all eligible lunchtime items! We are interested and passionate about preserving our environment, increasing environmental education and awareness at school and at home, and making your child's world a greener place to live. Harrington Reads Book Club

Now in its tenth year, Harrington Reads is a popular parent-child book group for fourth and fifth grade families that meets twice a year - in October and March. The program provides an opportunity for parents and children to come together for the purpose of shared reading and discussion.

METCO Family Friends

METCO Family Friends partners host families with our Boston families. Host families bring home Boston students one Thursday afternoon per month during the school year.

Science Enrichment

This PTA funded program brings grade-level, curriculum based science programming to all Harrington students.

Understanding Our Differences (UOD)

Understanding Our Differences (UOD) is a parent run disability awareness curriculum designed for the 3rd through 5th grades. Four parent volunteers from each classroom participate in a 30 minute training to lead students in activities designed to help them understand a variety of disabilities.

Sign up for UOD for 3rd grade, 4th grade, or 5th grade!
